## 2. How ProCaller Works

### 2.1 Service Overview

1. **You Acquire Domain Numbers:** Virtual domain numbers (not actual mobile numbers) through our platform
2. **You Add Information:** Business information, FAQs, and response data to your domain numbers
3. **AI Responds:** When people call or message your domain number via ProCaller app, our AI agents automatically respond
4. **Optional Contact Saving:** Users can save domain numbers as contacts within the app
5. **Local Notifications:** When saved domain numbers are accessed, the app provides local notifications

### 2.2 Important: We Do NOT Store Audio

**We do not record, store, or retain audio from calls.** We only store conversation summaries and analytics generated from interactions.

## 4. Data Storage

### 4.1 Local Device Storage

Some data is stored directly on your device for offline access and quick performance:

- Notification tracking and read/unread status

**Local Data:**
- Stored only on your device
- Not automatically synced to our servers
- Can be cleared through app settings
- Protected by your device's security features

### 4.2 Cloud Storage

Most data is securely stored in cloud infrastructure:

- Your account information
- Domain numbers and associated content
- Conversation summaries and analytics (no audio)
- Business information and AI response data

**Cloud Data:**
- Encrypted both in transit and at rest
- Stored in secure, industry-standard cloud facilities
- Protected by multiple layers of security
- Backed up regularly

## 8. Your Rights and Choices

### 8.1 Access and Export

You can:
- View all your data
- Export your data in downloadable formats
- Request copies of your information

**How:** Use in-app export features or contact rkverma@sherdeepai.com

### 8.2 Correction and Updates

You can:
- Update your profile and account information
- Modify domain content and response data
- Correct any inaccurate information

**How:** Use app editing features or contact us

### 8.3 Deletion

You can:
- Delete your account completely
- Remove specific domain numbers
- Clear local device data

**Account Deletion Process:**
1. Request deletion via app settings or email
2. **30-day grace period** where you can recover your account
3. After 30 days, permanent deletion within 7 days
4. Email confirmation at each stage

**What Gets Deleted:**
- All domain numbers and content
- All conversation summaries and analytics
- Your account and profile information
- All stored data

**What May Be Retained:**
- Anonymized analytics (no personal identifiers)
- Legal/financial records required by law
- Backup data (auto-deleted within 90 days)

### 8.4 Control Your Data

You can:
- Manage notification preferences
- Choose what data to save locally
- Configure retention periods for conversation history
- Opt-out of analytics collection
