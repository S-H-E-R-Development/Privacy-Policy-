# ProCaller - Data Deletion Request

**Last Updated:** January 19, 2026

---

## Delete Your Data

You can request deletion of some or all of your data from ProCaller without closing your account.

**Contact:** rkverma@sherdeepai.com  
**Processing Time:** Within 7 days

---

## What You Can Delete

You can request deletion of:

- **Conversation History** - All or specific conversation summaries and messages
- **Domain Numbers** - Individual or all domain numbers and their content
- **Analytics Data** - Usage statistics and performance metrics
- **Saved Contacts** - Domain numbers saved in your app
- **All Your Data** - Complete data deletion while keeping account active
- **Your Entire Account** - See our [Account Deletion Policy]('https://github.com/S-H-E-R-Development/Privacy-Policy-/blob/main/ProCaller%20Account%20Deletion%20Policy.md')

---

## How to Request Data Deletion

### Send an Email

**To:** rkverma@sherdeepai.com  
**Subject:** "Data Deletion Request"

**Include:**
- Your registered email address
- What data you want deleted
- Specific details (domain numbers, date ranges, etc.)

### Example Email

```
To: rkverma@sherdeepai.com
Subject: Data Deletion Request

Hello,

Please delete the following data from my account:

Email: myemail@example.com
Delete: All conversation history older than 6 months

Thank you
```

---

## Processing Timeline

1. **Send your request** to rkverma@sherdeepai.com
2. **We verify your identity** (usually within 24 hours)
3. **We process deletion** within 7 days
4. **You receive confirmation** via email when complete

---

## Important Notes

⚠️ **Deletions are permanent** - We cannot recover deleted data  
⚠️ **No refunds** - Deleting active domains doesn't trigger refunds  
⚠️ **Export first** - Download your data before requesting deletion if you want to keep a copy
