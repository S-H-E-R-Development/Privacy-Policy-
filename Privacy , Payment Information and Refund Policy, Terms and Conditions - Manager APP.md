## Privacy Policy

At **managelibs.com** by **Society of Humanity Education and Research**, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard data collected during the sign-up and registration process.

### Information We Collect:
- **Email Address:** Used for authentication and communication purposes.
- **Mobile Number:** Collected for registration, uniquely identifying the student, and app functionality.
- **Pin Code:** Collected to enhance app functionality based on location.
- **Library Owner Name & Library Name:** Used to personalize communication and facilitate library advertisement.
- **Number of Seats in Library:** Collected for app functionality such as seat booking.
- **Aspirant Name:** Collected during registration to maintain records.
- **Attendance Data:** We keep six months of attendance data recorded via our software to provide long-term records and analytics for both students and library managers.

### Use of Information:
- **Authentication & Communication:** Email and mobile numbers are used for account authentication and communication.
- **App Functionality:** The data facilitates features like live monitoring, aspirant registration, and attendance management.
- **Personalization:** Library-related information is used for customized services and advertising.
- **Record Keeping & Analytics:** Aspirant names and mobile numbers are used to maintain records and analyze attendance data for both students and library managers.

### Data Security:
We implement appropriate measures to safeguard your information. Your data is securely stored and only accessed by authorized personnel.

### Data Sharing:
We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. Data is only shared with trusted partners when necessary for service delivery.
